Find Your Perfect Mentor

Follow these 5 simple steps to get started.

Step 1

Set Your Goals

Before you start searching, take a moment to clarify exactly what you need from a mentor:

  • Are you preparing for college applications, scholarships, or standardized tests?
  • Do you need specialized help (e.g., essay writing, major selection)?
  • Are you looking for long-term guidance or short-term solutions?
Step 2

Use Filters to Narrow Your Search

Refine your mentor list using filters like:

  • School, major, and area of expertise
  • Pricing and availability
  • Past reviews and ratings
Step 3

Explore Mentor Profiles

  • Check their bio, education, and qualifications
  • Read reviews from other students
  • Compare experience levels and teaching styles
Step 4

Watch Mentor Videos

  • Watch their introduction video
  • Observe their communication style
  • Understand their personality and teaching approach
Step 5

Book a Lesson

  • Select a mentor that fits your needs
  • Choose a time that works for you
  • Lock in your session and get started!
